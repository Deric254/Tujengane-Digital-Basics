Hardware: The Physical Parts

Think of a computer like a kitchen: hardware are the appliances. Here we'll introduce the CPU, RAM, storage and the motherboard.

  • CPU (Central Processing Unit) — the brain that follows instructions.
  • RAM — short-term memory for running programs; faster but temporary.
  • Storage — long-term memory: HDD (spinning) and SSD (fast, no moving parts).
  • Motherboard & Power — the home where parts connect and receive power.
