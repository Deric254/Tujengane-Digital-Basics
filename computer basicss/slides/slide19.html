Intro to AI for Everyday Tasks

Artificial Intelligence (AI) can help you work faster, learn smarter, and automate simple tasks — even without programming skills.

  • What is AI? Tools that analyze data, understand language, or generate content (text, images, audio).
  • Everyday examples: auto-summarizing long notes, drafting emails, extracting key facts from a document, or generating simple images.
  • How it helps: saves time on repetitive work, offers writing and design suggestions, and assists decision making.

Safe & smart use

  1. Verify outputs—AI can be confident but wrong; always check facts.
  2. Protect sensitive data—don't paste private information into online tools.
  3. Combine AI with your judgment—use it to speed up, not replace critical thinking.
