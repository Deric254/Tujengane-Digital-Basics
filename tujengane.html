🎯 The 3 Rules of Clean Data

1

Consistency

Same format every time

Always use the same naming and structure.
2

Completeness

No missing information

Every field must be filled accurately.
3

Correct Format

Right data types

Use dates, numbers, or text appropriately.
If you follow these three rules, your data becomes usable instantly.

🏷️ Naming Files & Entries

Be Consistent. Be Clear.

  • Use full names
  • Avoid abbreviations
  • Stick to one format

🚫 Avoiding Blanks & Guesswork

Don't Fill Gaps with Zeros or Averages

  • Leads to false conclusions
